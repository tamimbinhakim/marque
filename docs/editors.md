## How to become an editor

Editors are not self-appointed. The path:

1. Author or co-author at least one Final MIP.
2. Sustained substantive engagement over ≥6 months.
3. Nomination by an existing editor.
4. Consensus approval by the current editor team.

Editors may be recused or removed per [GOVERNANCE.md](../GOVERNANCE.md).
